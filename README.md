# TradeArena
Welcome to TradeArena, the ultimate high-stakes financial simulator where seconds determine your success. Designed for both seasoned traders and market enthusiasts, TradeArena delivers a pulse-pounding, real-time competitive experience. Forget slow-moving portfolios; this is a battle of intuition, timing, and strategy played out in a sleek, dark-mode interface optimized for rapid-fire execution and elite performance.

In TradeArena, players enter custom-coded rooms like C6L7AY to face off in high-pressure trading heats. The game features a diverse, global selection of ten curated stocks, allowing you to diversify across the Indian Market (Hindalco, Coal India, Muthoot Finance, DMart, Ashok Leyland) and Global Tech Giants (NVIDIA, Salesforce, Pfizer, Nubank, Lyft). Whether you are betting on the volatility of semiconductors or the stability of industrial metals, every tick of the clock brings a new opportunity to gain the edge.

The core of the TradeArena challenge is the Cash Injection mechanic. Matches operate on a strict timer—often ten minutes—where players receive periodic capital boosts. Starting with $50,000, you will receive five subsequent injections, totaling a massive $250,000 to deploy. This creates a unique strategic layer: do you go "all-in" on early trends, or save your capital for a late-game market swing?

The platform provides a comprehensive suite of professional tools. The Market Dashboard offers a bird’s-eye view of price action and percentage changes, while the Interactive Charts allow you to visualize trends before they break. Use the Trade Desk to execute buy and sell orders instantly, adjusting share counts to fit your risk profile. Your live P&L (Profit and Loss) and Net Worth are tracked in real-time, feeding directly into the global Leaderboard where only the most disciplined traders rise to the top.

TradeArena isn't just about numbers; it is about outsmarting the room. Monitor your competitors, manage your liquidity, and master the art of the exit. The market is open, the capital is waiting, and the clock is ticking. Do you have what it takes to dominate the board?
